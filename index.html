At Taggart, we specialize in cross-border investments. Our insights and strategies connect businesses with global opportunities and elevate your market reach across continents.

What We Do

Taggart Advisors is an investment advisory firm that caters to mid-market transactions across the USA, Europe, and India in the industrial sector.

On the buy side, we work with manufacturing conglomerates, industrial holding companies, and private equity firms.

On the sell side, we collaborate with individual owners, banks, and private equity/financial holding companies to ensure seamless transactions.
